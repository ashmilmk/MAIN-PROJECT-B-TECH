Passage 1: The Happy Butterfly

Once there was a colorful butterfly. It flew from flower to flower in the garden, drinking nectar and enjoying the warm sunshine. The butterfly was very happy as it danced around the bright flowers. Every day, it fluttered joyfully, spreading beauty wherever it went.

Passage 2: Fun at the Park

Sarah and Tom went to the park one sunny afternoon. They swung on the swings and slid down the slides. They laughed together as they played on the green grass and watched other children running around. It was a fun and joyful day for Sarah and Tom at the park.

Passage 3: My Pet Fish

I have a pet fish named Bluey. Bluey swims around happily in a small glass tank in my room. Every morning, I feed Bluey some tiny food flakes and watch him swim quickly from side to side. He shines brightly under the water, and I love spending time watching him move.

Passage 4: The Lost Kitten

One sunny day, Sarah found a lost kitten in her backyard. The kitten was meowing softly and looked scared. Sarah carefully picked it up and gave it some milk. She petted its soft fur until it felt safe and warm. Sarah named her new friend Fluffy and promised to take care of her always.

Passage 5: The Magic Garden

In a small town, there was a big oak tree that hid something special behind it. One afternoon, Tommy and Lily discovered a magical garden filled with colorful flowers that sparkled in the sunlight. They saw butterflies fluttering and birds singing sweet songs. It was the most wonderful place they had ever seen, and they promised to visit again.
